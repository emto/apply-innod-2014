Innovative Design - Fall 2014 Video Tier Application

Thank you for your interest in joining Video Tier!

Video Tier is a new chapter for Innovative Design. Video Tier is centered on producing short promotional material for marketing student organizations and events. Since this is only the second semester of Video Tier, it's future and definition is really in your hands! The team will set its goals, pave their path, and have an awesome time making videos with each other. We are looking for candidates that are creative and enthusiastic about showcasing the world through a camera!

In addition to attending the 2 hour meetings and client events, members will have mini-projects to do just to explore new styles and types of videography. Members are also expected to participate in club-wide socials, workshops, and tabling.

Video Tier's meetings will be weekly for two hours with Brian. Along with this application, please paste links to sample work (Youtube or Vimeo preferred). Your sample work must reflect the specific role you are applying for, which are outlined below. Interviews will be given to second round candidates.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Roles (Choose First and Second choice):

- Camera/Photography: You will be the one behind the camera, taking the shots and directing the scene. The ideal candidate will have a videocamera/DSLR and good knowledge on how to work multi-angle shots.

- Graphics and Animation: You will be creating graphics and text animation for the video. The ideal candidate will have experience in graphic design (ex. previous Design Tier member), and knowledge of Adobe After Effects or Adobe Flash.

- Post-Production: You will be reviewing the footage, organizing, and pulling together the rest of the team's work to make the final production. The ideal candidate will have experience in either AVID, Final Cut Pro, or Adobe Premiere Pro.
