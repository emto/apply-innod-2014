Innovative Design - Fall 2014 Gold Tier Application

Welcome! You have chosen to apply to the Gold Tier where you can bond with fellow design team members on a weekly basis while creating designs for your peers and on-campus clients. Any experience in visual arts is highly preferred, but we also look for people with a passion for learning and enhancing their graphic design skills.

In addition to attending two-hour weekly team meetings and completing assignments outside of meeting times, team members are also expected to participate in club-wide socials, workshops, and tabling.

Portfolio details: Attach 3 – 5 samples of your work that best display your creativity, artistic skill, and/or design abilities. This could include photography, hand drawings, web designs, publication layouts, posters, fliers, etc. Please send as jpeg, png, or pdf. Do not compress files.
