Innovative Design - Fall 2014 Photo Tier Application

Thank you for your interest in joining the Photography Tier!

Photo Tier members work together to take event, studio, portrait, and stock photography for campus clients and local businesses. Passion for photography is a must and previous experience is definitely a plus. InnoD's Photo Tier has plenty to offer if you're committed, willing to learn, and just have some fun.

In addition to attending the 2 hour meetings and client events, members will have mini-projects to do just to explore new styles and types of photography. Members are also expected to participate in club-wide socials, workshops, and tabling.

Along with this application, please attach your photography portfolio of 5 photos in JPEG.
