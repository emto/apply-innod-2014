Blue Tier Fall 2014 Application

Hello! Thank you for your interest in Blue Tier! Blue Tier is a fast-paced and awesome design team. We handle design requests from logos to banners to flyers from clients both off and on campus. If you have intermediate to advance skill level in design, we would love for you to apply.

The application consists of the questions below as well as a required 3-5 design pieces to showcase your skill level. Please have your designs in jpeg, png, or pdf. Do not compress files. There is a place to submit a link to your portfolio at the end of the application.

If you have any questions, feel free to send an email over to innovativedesignatcal@gmail.com
